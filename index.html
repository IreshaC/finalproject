About us

Welcome to Crystal Auto Detailing, your premier destination for top-notch auto detailing services in Nugegoda, Sri Lanka. At Crystal Auto Detailing, we understand that your vehicle is more than just a mode of transportation—it's a reflection of your personality and a testament to your lifestyle. Who we are

Believing that customer satisfaction is the first and foremost priority which leads to perfection, we focus on providing the finest quality, innovative auto detailing solutions enriched with integrity and ultimate care.

Surface Protection

Crystal Auto Detailing offers two different kinds of paint protection procedures which can be used singularly or even as a combination to provide the ultimate and prolonged protection.

Detailing

Paint Correction is a multi-step machine polishing process to restore and rejuvenate the paintwork of a vehicle with the help of special compounds and polishes by removing surface imperfections.

Services

Interior Detailing

Interior detailing refers to the meticulous cleaning, restoration, and finishing of a vehicle's interior. This process involves thorough cleaning and maintenance of all interior components to enhance the vehicle's appearance and ensure a pleasant driving experience.

Exterior Detailing

Crystal Auto Detailing offers quality assured Multi-Stage Paint Correction which results in a flawless, defect free finish while eliminating swirl marks or heavier paint defects. This multi stage procedure includes many levels of paint correction done by varying grades of compound and polishes along with varying time duration of machine correction.

Quick Detailing

Quick detailing is a fast, efficient process of cleaning and maintaining the appearance of a vehicle, focusing on the most visible and frequently touched areas.

Surface Protection

The newest technology in the automotive industry for paint protection is the Nano Ceramic Coating which involves the application of a unique formula of ceramic molecular compounds with advanced chemical nanotechnology, creating a protective layer over the vehicle’s factory paint.
